Catastrophe Ward

Welcome to CataWard - your Disaster Preparedness Hub! Stay informed and ready with our essential tips and guidelines for navigating natural disasters.

Typhoon

Know what to do before, during and after typhoons.

Flood

Prepare for a flood by getting an emergency kit, a family communication plan, safe shelters, and evacuating when told.

Earthquake

During an earthquake, drop, cover, and hold on until shaking stops.

Landslide

Move away from the landslide path or seek high ground.

Tsunami

Listen to local alerts and evacuate to higher ground or inland if a tsunami is coming.
